⭐ Featured Snack of the Day

Vada Pav – Mumbai's Pride

Often called "India's Burger," Vada Pav is a spiced potato fritter (vada) tucked inside a soft bread roll (pav), served with dry garlic chutney, green chutney, and fried green chilli. It's cheap, filling, and absolutely explosive in flavor — no wonder it feeds millions on Mumbai streets every single day.

Our Story

What is Chakh लो?

"Chakkh" means to taste in Hindi — and that's exactly what Chakh लो is all about. We are a passionate platform for food lovers who want to explore, discover, and celebrate the incredible world of snacks and street food.

From fiery Mumbai street stalls to cozy grandma kitchens, from fast food joints to traditional sweet shops — Chakh लो brings every bite to your fingertips. Explore recipes, discover new cuisines, and satisfy your cravings with us.
