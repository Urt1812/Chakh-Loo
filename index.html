🏞️ Our Roots

The Pride of Bihar & Mithila

Bihar is the land of ancient wisdom, the Ganges, and some of India's most soul-satisfying foods. Litti Chokha is the beating heart of Bihari cuisine — a rustic, fire-kissed dish that has fed generations.

Mithila — the ancient kingdom of King Janaka and birthplace of Sita — is famous for its art, culture, and food. Thekua, the beloved sweet of Chhath Puja, and crispy Nimki are the proud flavours of Mithila.

We bring these authentic flavours from the heart of Bihar & Mithila straight to Delhi — made fresh every day, cooked only in Pure Desi Ghee, exactly the way it has always been.
